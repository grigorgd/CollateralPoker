<h2>Collateral Poker</h2>
<h3>Usage</h3>

<p style="line-height:150%;">The game starts by creating a server  Game->Create 
and joining the game Game->Join or by joining the game created by another user 
Game->Join and imput username, port number and server ip address. After joining 
seats will be enable for players. If seat is taken, it will be marked by username 
of user who took it and disabled for remaining users. Along with the seat occupation, 
Play button appears. By pressing Play button, player informs other players that he 
is ready to play and color of his username changes from white to green. To starts 
game all players have to press Play button and at least two seats have to be taken. 
If one of players left his seat, remaining users have to confirm that they are 
ready again.</p>

<p style="line-height:150%;">Overview window is shown to players after each round. Next round starts 
when all players confirm round results and close overview window. All seats are available to seat 
for players again after last round.</p>

<ul style="list-style-type:square; line-height:150%;">
<li>Game->Create - creates server on this machine ip address</li>
<li>Game->Join - new player joins game</li>
<li>Game->Close client - closes this client connection with server</li>
<li>Game->Info - oppen message window about users and the game</li>
<li>Game->Exit - closes program<li>
<li>Options->Four color deck - cards will be shown in four-color suits</li>
<li>Options->Sound - enables sound</l>
<li>Options->Next back - changes back suit</li>
<li>Help->Rules - shows rules window</li>
</ul>

<h3>Rules</h3>

<p style="line-height:150%;">Collateral poker is a game for 2 to 4 players. Deck used for the game consists 24 cards ('9' to 'Ace' 
for every suit). Poker hands are used in the game and in terms of streght these are:</p>

<ul style="list-style-type:square; line-height:150%;">
<li>high card</li>
<li>pair</li>
<li>two pairs</li>
<li>three of a kind</li>
<li>straight</li>
<li>flush</li>
<li>full hause</li>
<li>four of a kind</li>
<li>straight flush</li>
</ul>

<p style="line-height:150%;">The game is about declaring poker hands, raising them and checking hands declared by rivals. The 
goal of the player is to force opponents to draw cards. Starting player is chosen randomly. At the 
begining each player receives one card. Player who starts a round has to declare poker hand because 
there is no hand to call yet. Next player has two options. He can call bet of previous player or 
raise and declare higher hand. The order of the players is clockwise in the occupied seats. 
Each round lasts until one of the players decide to call.</p>

<p style="line-height:150%;">If player call, all players' cards are revealed. If declared hand is possible to dispose from 
all players' cards, then the player who call will start next round with extra card. If declared 
hand is not possible to dispose then extra card is given to player who declare hand. If any of 
the playerscollects more than five cards, he looses and leaves the game. The last player 
remaining in the game wins.</p>
